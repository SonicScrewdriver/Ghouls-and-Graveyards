YOUR TURN:• Your first click establishes your home base.• You have three lives.• On each turn you can move up, down, left or   right up to a max of:    First life: 8 steps    Second life: 6 steps    Third life: 4 steps    Carrying the treasure (all lives): 4 steps• Hitting a wall immediately ends your turn.• Moving within three spaces of the treasure   awakens the ghost. This doesn't end your turn.• Picking up the treasure immediately ends your turn.• Running into the ghost while empty-handed ends your   turn, loses a life and returns you to your home base.• Running into the ghost while carrying the treasure—   outside of the treasure room—ends the game!
